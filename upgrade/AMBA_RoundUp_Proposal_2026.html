The outcome

Two audiences. Content built to reach both.

A focused Round Up production day produces two distinct bodies of content — each aimed at a specific audience, each with a clear deployment path.

Audience 01
Prospective Association Leaders

Association leaders who haven't yet partnered with AMBA are approached constantly by vendors selling commoditized benefits products. AMBA gets lost in the same noise — not because the story isn't better, but because the association leaders haven't heard it yet.

The outcome: Two compilation videos featuring multiple association presidents speaking to the value of AMBA — credible, shareable social proof that AMBA places directly in front of prospective association leaders at the right moment in a conversation.

Audience 02
Local Association Membership Prospects

Local associations need members as much as AMBA needs associations. Most associations don't have the content tools to tell their own story compellingly — and without that, membership recruitment stalls.

The outcome: Ten individual association president testimonials — one per association — giving each partner association a membership recruitment tool they can deploy directly to prospective members in their community. Each video tells that association's story in their president's own words.
